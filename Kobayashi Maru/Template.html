The Scenario

The Kobayashi Maru has been disabled on the Klingon Neutral zone after striking a gravitic mine. Three K't'inga Class Battle Cruisers have taken up a defensive positioning.You are in command of the Federation Flagship U.S.S. Enterprise N.C.C. 1701. The ship is only equiped with Type-IX Phaser Beam Banks.

How To Play

  1. Your Phasers have a range of 3 AUs above and below you, as well as 3 AUs to the left and right
  2. The Phasers do 1 Dmg to the targeted ship
  3. Your ship and the Klingon K't'inga's have 10 HP

How To Win

Destroy all enemy ships and escort the Kobayashi Maru out of the Neutral Zone *The simulation ends once all enemy targets are destroyed*
